METANOIA

Metanoia (Team Metanoia) is a kpop dance cover group with 4 members, Bianca, Leira, Samantha, and Ariane. Metanoia debuted on June 6, 2020 with MAMAMOO's HIP.
After a few months, they added new members Jholan and Allyza, continuing the group with 6 members.
